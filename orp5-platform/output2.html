About ORP-5

Building on a strong legacy of international symposia held in France (2012), Italy (2015), Brazil (2018), and Japan (2023), the 5th International Conference on Organic and Natural Rice Production Systems (ORP-5) is coming to India.

The conference brings together scientists, farmers, policymakers, and industry stakeholders to advance sustainable, climate-resilient rice production systems globally.

Conference Themes

Explore the 9 core thematic areas shaping the future of organic agriculture.

Theme 1

Organic and Natural Rice Production Systems – Current Status

Assessment of existing organic and natural rice production practices, Field performance, productivity, and yield stability, and farmer-level experiences across diverse agro-ecologies.

Submit to this theme
Theme 2

Innovations and Emerging Technologies in Organic Rice Production Systems

Innovative organic technologies, bio-inputs, biofertilizers, and biopesticides, best practices, cost effective farmers innovations models and successful international case studies

Submit to this theme
Theme 3

Natural Farming Models for Sustainable Rice Production

Zero-budget natural farming, ecological and regenerative rice farming approaches, region-specific natural farming practices, Integration of livestock and diversified farming systems

Submit to this theme
Theme 4

Climate Change Adaptation and Carbon-Neutral Rice Production Systems

Climate-resilient production systems, mitigation strategies, carbon budgeting, and low-emission rice systems.

Submit to this theme
Theme 5

Soil, Water and Plant Health Management

Soil fertility enhancement and organic nutrient management, soil microbiome and biological soil health, water-efficient and alternate wetting and drying (AWD) systems, carbon sequestration, and integrated pest, disease, and weed management.

Submit to this theme
Theme 6

Food Quality, Nutrition and Human Health

Grain quality, food safety, nutritional attributes, residue-free production, and traceability, health impacts and consumer perceptions, value addition and fortified organic rice

Submit to this theme
Theme 7

AI-Driven Mechanization and Digital Intelligence for Organic Rice Production Systems

Leveraging Artificial Intelligence, Data Analytics, IoT, Precision Farming, and Smart Decision Support Systems for Sustainable Organic Rice Production Systems

Submit to this theme
Theme 8

Scaling, Value Chains, and Market Opportunities

Scaling up organic and natural rice production systems, sustainable supply chains and market linkages, branding, certification, and traceability systems, value chain integration, export potential and domestic market development

Submit to this theme
Theme 9

Policy, Institutions, and Capacity Building-Youth & Farmers Perspectives

Policy frameworks, institutional support mechanisms, certification standards and regulatory challenges, capacity building, extension, and farmer training, role of youth, startups and entrepreneurship in organic rice production systems

Submit to this theme
